Introduction¶

This dataset contains more than 10,000 movies with their revenues, budgets, popularity, user ratings and many other aspects that we will use to figure out some patterns related to movies and find insights that will help us determine the measures of success for movies in the industry. This dataset was downloaded from Kaggle where it was collected from The Movie Database (TMDb)

The questions that we think are best to imply to get sufficient insights out of this dataset are: 1) Which months have better revenue? Is it associated with holidays and seasons? 2) What are the properties that affect the proftability of the movies?
