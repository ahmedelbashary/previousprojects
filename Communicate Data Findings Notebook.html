Introduction¶

In this day of age with everything starting to be modernized and advanced specially when it comes to transportation, cars and buses aren't the only way to get around anymore. This takes us to the modern idea of bike sharing that we will be analyzing today.

What is bike sharing? A bicycle-sharing system, public bicycle scheme or public bike share (PBS) scheme, is a service in which bicycles are made available for shared use to individuals on a short term basis for a price or free. Many bike share systems allow people to borrow a bike from a "dock" and return it at another dock belonging to the same system. Docks are special bike racks that lock the bike, and only release it by computer control. The user enters payment information, and the computer unlocks a bike. The user returns the bike by placing it in the dock, which locks it in place.

That is why we will be analyzing the dataset of Ford GoBike which is a bike sharing provider through exploratory analysis until we get to the explanatory analysis

What is the structure of your dataset?¶

The dataset contains 1,863,721 rows and 16 columns. It consists of the information gathered about the Ford GoBike trips done in 2018. The information is the duration for each trip, start time, end time and all the information of the start station and end station. Also, it includes all the information needed about the bike and the user. The user if they are a member or not, what's their birthdate and their gender.

What is/are the main feature(s) of interest in your dataset?¶

First of all, I think the main features are the duration of each trip, the age and the mean age of the users and how does that affect the duration. Also, the time of year that most likely has more trips or less trips, but this will need more columns that are not yet done, but will be done. Moreover, the gender of the users and how mainly dominates the number of trips. This will all be checked thoroughly in order to help this business thrive and reach more target audience.

What features in the dataset do you think will help support your investigation into your feature(s) of interest?¶

As mentioned above the duration, start and end times, the gender, age and subscribers or not. All of these will be used to get to the insights that we want to know.
